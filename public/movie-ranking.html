Best Anime According To Scotty

My top 3 Anime of all-time!

Demon Slayer: All Arcs

Demon Slayer is an incredibly engaging anime. The animation is beautiful, the characters are unique and engaging, and the diversity of personalities makes it a great anime!

Juijustsu Kaisen

Similar to Demon Slayer, the characters of Jujustsu Kaisen are unique, the animation is unique, and the overall "cool guy" vibe of the anime makes it a fun watch. Ninjas, epic fights, and mysterious antagos are all part of the story that flies by so fast I can't help but watch it again just for the rush of the experience.

Mob Psycho 100

Unlike Demon Slayer and Juijustsu Kaisen, Mob Psycho 100 is hilarious, extremely meta, and a lot of action. The animation is goofy (if not intentionally bad) and the story allows that animation and humor to be enjoyable despite being a bit of a mess. Laughs galore, cool action sequences, and real character growth Mob Psycho 100 is a must watch.
